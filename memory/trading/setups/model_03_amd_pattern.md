## üìà Trade Management

### Entry Options

**Standard Entry:**
- After reclaim confirmed
- At market or limit
- Full position

**Aggressive Entry:**
- On wick (before reclaim)
- Higher risk
- Better price

**Conservative Entry:**
- Wait for structure
- Micro BOS confirmation
- Later entry, higher probability

### Stop Placement

**Standard:**
- Below manipulation wick (longs)
- Above manipulation wick (shorts)
- 5-10 pip buffer

**Tight:**
- Below Asia Low (if wick is close)
- Risk of being stopped by noise

### Target Management

**Conservative:**
- Target 1: Asia High/Low (close 100%)
- Done for the day

**Aggressive:**
- Target 1: Asia level (close 50%)
- Target 2: Next liquidity (close 50%)
- Capture extended move
