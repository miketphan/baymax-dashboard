## üìà Trade Management

### Entry

**Aggressive:**
- Enter as price reclaims
- Better price
- Slightly lower win rate

**Conservative:**
- Wait for 5M candle close
- Worse price
- Higher win rate

### Stop

**Tight:**
- Below/above sweep wick
- 10-15 pips
- Higher risk of noise

**Standard:**
- Below/above pre-market extreme
- 15-20 pips
- More breathing room

### Target & Exit

**Rule:** EXIT BY 10:10 AM

**Options:**
1. Fixed target (1:2 or 1:3)
2. Trail stop
3. Close at 10:10 regardless

**Never:** Hold past 10:10 AM
