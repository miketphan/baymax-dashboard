## üéØ What is Killzone Continuation?

**Core Principle:**
> "Trend + Killzone + Value = High Probability"

## üìà Trade Management

### Entry Timing

**Aggressive:**
- Enter at first touch of zone
- Better price
- Higher risk

**Conservative:**
- Wait for HL/LH to form
- Wait for micro BOS
- Later entry, higher win rate

### Stop Placement

**Standard:**
- Below pullback low (longs)
- Above pullback high (shorts)
- 15-25 pips typical

**Tight:**
- Below LTF swing
- 10-15 pips
- Higher risk of stop

### Target & Exit

**Fixed:**
- Target next HTF level
- Close full position

**Scaled:**
- 50% at 1:2
- 25% at 1:3
- 25% trail
