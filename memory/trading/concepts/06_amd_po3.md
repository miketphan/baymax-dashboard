## ğŸ“ How to Trade AMD

### Step 1: Mark Asia Range (8 PM - 12 AM)
```
At midnight EST (or when you wake up):
1. Look at 8 PM - 12 AM price action
2. Mark the highest price = Asia High
3. Mark the lowest price = Asia Low
4. Note which side has liquidity (EQH/EQL nearby)
```

### Step 2: Wait for Manipulation (2 AM - 6 AM)
```
During London session:
1. Watch for sweep of Asia High OR Low
2. Must be a clear break with wick
3. Look for immediate rejection
4. Price should reclaim range within 1-2 candles
```

### Step 3: Enter on Reclaim (After sweep)
```
For Longs (swept Asia Low):
1. Price breaks below Asia Low
2. Immediate bullish reversal
3. Close back above Asia Low
4. Enter on next bullish candle
5. Stop below manipulation wick

For Shorts (swept Asia High):
1. Price breaks above Asia High
2. Immediate bearish reversal
3. Close back below Asia High
4. Enter on next bearish candle
5. Stop above manipulation wick
```

### Step 4: Target Distribution (10 AM - 11 AM)
```
Targets:
- Bullish: Target Asia High, then next resistance
- Bearish: Target Asia Low, then next support

Take profit:
- Scale out at Asia High/Low
- Trail stop for extended move
- Close by 11 AM (end of NYKZ)
```
